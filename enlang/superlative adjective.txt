a superlative adjective is used to compare three or more objects, people, or places.

superlative form takes a comparison to the highest degree possible.

example: mom's apple pie is the best dessert at the party.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
how to construct:

one-syllable word:
	add "est" to the word
	long - longest
	big  – biggest

one-syllable word ending with an "e":
	add "st" to the word
	fine - finest

two-syllable word ending with a "y":
	change "y" to "i" and add "est" to the word
	pretty – prettiest

Words that contain two or three syllables:
	preceded by the words "most" or "least".
	most  handsome
	least perfect
