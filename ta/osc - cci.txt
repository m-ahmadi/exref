commodity channel index

a momentum-based oscillator
it's possible to normalize an oscillator by dividing the values by a constant divisor
while:
	originally developed for commodities, also used for stock/index/futures/options
	20 days most common, it can vary to adjust sensitivity
developed by donald lambert

cci:
	compares current price with a moving average over a selected time span (usually 20 days)
	values are normalized by using a divisor based on mean deviation
	fluctuates in a constant range from +100 upside to -100 downside

buy: 	values above +100
sell: 	values below -100

cci as overbought/oversold oscillator
	above +100:	overbought
	below -100:	oversold
