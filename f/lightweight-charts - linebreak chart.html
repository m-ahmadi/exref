upward   line forms when price closes above highest high of previous 3 linebreak bars
downward line forms when price closes below lowest  low  of previous 3 linebreak bars

the decision uses close price from underlying time-based candles (1m, 5m, 1h, ...)
the threshold levels come from the last 3 linebreak bars

pseudo code:

H3 = highest high of last 3 lines
L3 = lowest  low  of last 3 lines

if   close > H3:
  new up   line
elif close < L3:
  new down line
else:
  no new line

linebreak bars are not fixed in time, they only appear when price breaks out of prior structure.

how the first line is created when there's no linebreak bars yet:
  1. take first available close from underlying timeframe (e.g., 1m, 5m)
  2. watch subsequent closes
  3. when close > previous close, first up   line forms
  4. when close < previous close, first down line forms
  5. once 3 lines exist, normal 3-line reversal rule starts applying
