cell		basic unit of life, all living organisms are composed of cells
		a cytoplasm inside membrane, which contains dna, rna, ...
		unicellular:	single cell organisms
		multicellular:	multi  cell organisms

gene		traits are inherited through gene transmission
		genes are located on chromosomes and consist of dna

evolution	any genetic change in a population that is inherited over several generations
		these changes may be small or large, noticeable or not so noticeable

homeostasis	ability to maintain a constant internal environment in response to environmental changes

thermodynamics	energy is constant and energy transformation is not completely efficient
