english has 15 vowel sounds represented by the letters a, e, i, o, u, y, w gh.

3 categories of vowel sounds:

long (vowels that sound like the letter name):
	a	cake
	e	keep
	i	bike
	o	home
	u	cute
	
short (the most common sound for a single vowel spelling):
	a	cat
	e	bed
	i	sit
	o	top
	u	sun

other (the remaining vowel sounds):
	u	put
	oo	soon
	aw	dog
	oi	join
	ow	down
