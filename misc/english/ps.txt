used to express afterthoughts (something you forgot to mention in main body)

abbr		latin			meaning			preferred in
================================================================================
p.s.		postscriptum		written after		us
ps		...			...			uk

(express further afterthoughts with pps, ppps, ...)

examples
	PS You suck.
	P.S. You suck.
	PS: You suck.
	P.s.: You suck.
